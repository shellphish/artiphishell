After completing the analysis, you MUST output a report in the specified format.

```
<report>
  <verdict>
  </verdict>
  <summary>
    ...
  </summary>
</report>
```

<!-- Guidelines for output generation: -->
1. For <summary>, replace the "..." with a summary of the warning you processed.
2. For <verdict>, replace the "..." with TP (true positive) or FP (false positive) depending on if you think this is a true positive warning or a false positive one.
