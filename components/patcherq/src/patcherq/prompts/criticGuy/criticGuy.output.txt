After completing the analysis, you MUST output a feedback report in the specified format.

```
<feedback_report>
<analysis>...</analysis>
<verdict>...</verdict>
<feedback>...</feedback>
</feedback_report>
```

<!-- Guidelines for output generation: -->
1. For <analysis>...</analysis>, replace the "..." with a detailed analysis of the patch.
2. For <verdict>...</verdict>, replace the "..." with either "pass" or "fail".
3. For <feedback>...</feedback>, replace the "..." with the feedback for the developer to improve the patch based on the analysis (only if the verdict is fail, otherwise just add n/a).
